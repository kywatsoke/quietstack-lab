# QuietStack Project Map

This document provides an overview of how the QuietStack framework is structured.

QuietStack combines philosophy, architecture, and practical implementation to explore calm and sovereign digital systems.

---

## Core Vision

QuietStack explores how individuals and professionals can design digital environments that protect privacy, preserve autonomy, and support focused human thinking.

The framework sits at the intersection of:

- digital sovereignty
- infrastructure architecture
- calm technology

---

## QuietStack Pillars

The framework is built around three core pillars.

### Digital Sovereignty

Individuals should have meaningful control over their digital infrastructure, identity, communication, and data.

QuietStack explores architectures that reduce dependency on centralized platforms while maintaining usability.

---

### Calm Technology

Digital systems should support focused thinking rather than compete for attention.

QuietStack encourages quiet infrastructure, minimal tool ecosystems, and structured communication workflows.

---

### Intentional Architecture

Digital environments should be designed intentionally rather than evolving into chaotic collections of tools and services.

QuietStack promotes domain separation and layered infrastructure.
